About Us

Welcome to the Liloan National High School Library!

We’re proud to be a central part of our school community, dedicated to fostering a love for reading and supporting our students' learning journeys.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an inviting space where students can explore a variety of resources, work together, and develop critical thinking skills. We aim to empower every student to become a lifelong learner, ready for future challenges.

Our History

Since our establishment in 2007, the library has grown and evolved to meet the needs of our students and community. We’ve expanded our collection and updated our facilities to ensure everyone has access to the latest information and technology, while also celebrating our local culture and history.

Our Facilities

Our library is designed to be a welcoming and comfortable place for all students. With cozy reading areas, study rooms, and computer stations, it’s perfect for individual study or group projects. We provide modern technology, including Wi-Fi and multimedia resources, to enhance the learning experience.

Programs and Services

We offer a variety of programs and services to support our students:

  • Reading Programs: Enjoy book fairs, reading challenges, and author visits to inspire a love for reading.
  • Research Assistance: Our friendly librarians are here to help with research projects and study strategies.
  • Workshops: We host workshops on topics like information literacy and study skills to help students succeed academically.
  • Community Engagement: We partner with local organizations to promote literacy and educational initiatives outside of school.
Our Commitment to Excellence

We are dedicated to continuously improving our services based on student and faculty feedback. Our library staff works hard to create a supportive and engaging environment where students can learn and grow.

Join Us

Whether you need a quiet place to study, want to explore new books, or require help with research, the Liloan National High School Library is here for you. We encourage all students to take advantage of our resources and participate in our programs. Together, let’s embark on an exciting journey of discovery and learning!
